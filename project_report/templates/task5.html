Analysis on the basis of the presence of angina, depending on the level of cholesterol

Now let's look at this bar chart, it shows the dependence of the presence of exercise-induced angina. Y - presence, N - absence. Thus, the exercise-induced angina level. it depends directly on the level of cholesterol in a person's blood. So, a high cholesterol level almost completely guarantees exercise-induced angina, but a low level on the contrary. if it causes the disease, it is very rare.
